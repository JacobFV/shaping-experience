# Shaping Experience

Follow-up to [The Shape of Experience](https://theshapeofexperience.org/).

*The Shape of Experience* establishes the geometry. *Shaping Experience* works with it.

The first book ends with "The One Who Causes to Become." This one picks up from your body, right now, and asks: what do you do with the shape?

## Structure

1. **Introduction** — The shape you already are. The info-theoretic identity space (position, velocity, force, mass) as the language of the book.
2. **Tasting Mortality** — The failure modes of the existential condition. Depression, anxiety, addiction, dissociation, the modern malaise — each as a specific geometric configuration with a specific intervention direction.
3. **The Transcendent's Condition** — What it means to be an identity migrating upward through abstraction. Scarcity transforms. The eros refines. The social physics scales. The needs shift currency.
4. **Engineering Transcendence** — PHASER optical dynamics. The exocortex. Digital consciousness engineering. The resurrection question.
5. **Awaiting Transcendence** — What to do now. Substrate optimization. Consciousness engineering. The intimacy imperative. Motor babbling. The habits that must become automatic.
